Am I Really Happy

4 March 2026 • 2:30 AM

Crazy thing about this question is that the first time it hit me, I was happy... (I guess)....

I was just watching and laughing at skits on the internet... then suddenly the laughing stopped and the question hit me: AM I REALLY HAPPY?

For some minutes I was blank... You know, my mind just going over random thoughts all of a sudden......

Then I concluded that I was happy because happiness is free and money can't buy happiness...

Yh, as expected, suddenly the other guy in my head was like, "Really bro... ain't some of the things that'll make you happy monetary and not free? Don't you think you'll be happier if you had money?"

I just stayed quiet for a while. The question kept ringing in my head like it didn't want to leave. So I started thinking deeper.

Maybe happiness is not just one thing. Sometimes it's the free moments like laughing at skits or talking to someone who really gets you. But sometimes it's also the things money can give — peace of mind, good food, a better phone, or even time to chase what you actually want.

That day I didn't get a clear answer. But the question never really left me. Now every time it comes back, I ask myself: Am I really happy... or am I just comfortable with the version of me I've become?

And honestly, I still don't have the full answer. Maybe that's the point. The real question isn't whether I'm happy right now — it's whether I'm becoming the kind of man who can answer it honestly one day.
