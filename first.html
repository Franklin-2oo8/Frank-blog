How I changed at 15
(My first blog)

27 February 2026 • 9:30 PM

Hey y'all, once again WELCOME to my blog (Frank's Life).

So it all started when I turned 15 — that was when I discovered... uhh my bad, I totally forgot to introduce myself properly...

Well like I said, my name is Frank and I'm Nigerian. I'm a front-end web developer and currently a student chasing a BSc in computer science.

Yh, back to what I was saying. When I turned 15, that was when I discovered that I was no longer me.

I noticed a series of changes going on in my body, my character yh... especially my character — I mean the way I behaved and related with people generally.

I changed from the guy that used to talk too much (according to them)... personally I don't think I talked too much... to the guy that does not talk unless necessary (according to people I'm too proud of myself, I don't talk to other people cos I look less of them). Well I don't look less of people, it's just that I.... Nevermind.

I suddenly knew how to hide my emotions, especially anger. I mean I could be really mad at you and still stay calm and maybe just smile or stay silent.

I also started overthinking things and even questioning things like.. I remember a time where I was watching skits online and was laughing then suddenly the question hit me Am I really happy

And lastly that was when I met My First Love
