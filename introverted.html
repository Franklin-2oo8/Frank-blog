How Did I Get So Introverted

1 March 2026 • 9:30 AM

Hmmm this is a question I really can't answer myself I mean I can't really remember when it started or even how it started.

I can vividly remember when I used to be the guy that talk too much (according to people)... personally I don't think I talked too much... to the guy that does not talk unless necessary (according to people I'm too proud of myself, I don't talk to other people cos I look less of them).

Yh, I don't really look less of people... it's just that I prefer keeping to myself these days.

I can hold my peace even when someone is provoking me bad. I just smile or stay silent and move on with my day.

That's why I prefer having one close friend instead of many casual ones. Talking too much now tire me.

And honestly I chat with AI more than humans sometimes because
it feels deeper and there's no judgement.

Looking back now, I think this is just who I am becoming — the quiet guy who doesn't chase noise and just focuses on becoming a better man every single day.

The end.
